# Privacy Policy — Zabbix AppleTV Dashboard

Zabbix AppleTV Dashboard is a network-monitoring display app for Apple TV. It renders
dashboards from a Zabbix server you control natively for wall-mounted or kiosk-style
viewing. It is distributed privately to individual organizations as a Custom App via
Apple Business Manager or Apple School Manager, not through the public App Store.

## Data collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data, and includes no
analytics, tracking, or advertising of any kind.

## Server credentials

The Zabbix server URL, username, and password you enter during setup are stored only
on the Apple TV device itself, using its secure Keychain. These credentials are never
transmitted anywhere except directly to the Zabbix server you configure, for the sole
purpose of authenticating that connection.

## Network communication

The app communicates exclusively with the Zabbix server address you provide. It makes
no connections to the developer, to Apple, or to any third party, and no data about
you, your organization, or your Zabbix server is transmitted to anyone else.

## Questions

For questions about this policy or the app's behavior, contact the IT department of
the organization that deployed this app to your device.
